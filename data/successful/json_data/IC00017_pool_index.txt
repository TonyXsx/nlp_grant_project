Lead Applicant DetailsUnmet Patient NeedOsteoporosis is a debilitating disease characterised by loss of bone mass and deterioration of the bone structure,resulting in a significantly increased risk of fragility fractures. An estimated 17% of men and 34% of women over 50 are affected by osteoporosis worldwide[1], with resulting fragility fractures accounting for significant health and social care costs. In 2017, fragility fractures cost the UK £4. 5bn with an associated one-year mortality rate of 33% –and such fractures are set to rise by 27% by 2030[2]. Osteoporosis is highly treatable, but drastically under-detected. Often those at high risk of fracture are not identified or treated, with an estimated treatment gap of 66% for women in the UK[2]. IBEX Bone Health (BH)will accurately predict osteoporosis from wrist x-rays opportunistically taken using widely available equipment whenever patients are x-rayed for other indications.
