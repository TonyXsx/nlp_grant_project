Researchers have done studies called clinical trials to test treatments for heavy periods. In these clinical trials, researchers measure how much blood a woman loses during her period (called \"menstrual blood loss\"). Then they measure it again after she has had treatment to see if the treatment has helped. Researchers can measure menstrual blood loss in different ways.
